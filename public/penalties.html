Penalty Calculation Rules

  • Undergraduate Students: ₹5 per day
  • Postgraduate Students: ₹5 per day
  • Research Scholars: ₹5 per day
  • Faculty Members: ₹5 per day
  • Maximum penalty: ₹500 per book
  • Grace period: 3 days (no penalty)
